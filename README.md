GlobeTrotter – Empowering Personalized Travel Planning

GlobeTrotter is a smart, user-centric travel planning platform designed to simplify and enhance the way people plan multi-city trips. It enables users to create personalized itineraries, manage budgets, explore destinations and activities, visualize travel timelines, and share trips with others—all in one place.

Overall Vision

The vision of GlobeTrotter is to become an intelligent, collaborative, and personalized travel companion that transforms trip planning into an engaging and stress-free experience. The platform empowers users to dream, design, and organize journeys with complete visibility, making travel planning as exciting as the journey itself.

Mission

The mission of this project is to build a responsive, intuitive, and feature-rich travel planning application that:

Simplifies multi-city trip planning

Helps users manage itineraries and budgets effectively

Provides clear visualization of travel plans

Encourages sharing and collaboration

The solution combines a well-structured relational database with a modern frontend interface to deliver a smooth end-to-end experience.

Problem Statement

Design and develop a complete travel planning application where users can:

Create customized multi-city itineraries

Assign travel dates, activities, and budgets

Discover destinations and activities through search

View cost breakdowns and visual calendars

Share trip plans publicly or with friends

The system must demonstrate proper use of relational databases to manage complex travel data and support dynamic, user-driven interfaces.
